  • Social Media

    Social media has evolved significantly over the years, transforming the way we communicate and connect. Here's a brief overview:

  • History of Social Media

    Social media has its roots in early forms of online communication, such as bulletin board systems (BBS) in the 1970s and 1980s. The emergence of platforms like Six Degrees in the late 1990s paved the way for modern social networking. However, it was Friendster, launched in 2002, that popularized the concept. Subsequently, platforms like MySpace, LinkedIn, Facebook, and Twitter contributed to the explosive growth of social media.

  • Examples of Social Media Platforms

  • Facebook: Founded in 2004 by Mark Zuckerberg, Facebook is the largest social networking platform with billions of users worldwide.
  • Twitter: Launched in 2006, Twitter allows users to share short messages called tweets, fostering real-time conversations.
  • Instagram: Acquired by Facebook in 2012, Instagram focuses on visual content, allowing users to share photos and videos.
  • LinkedIn: Launched in 2003, LinkedIn is a professional networking platform connecting individuals in the business world.
